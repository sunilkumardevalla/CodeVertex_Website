Colorado Clinical Booking

Book licensed clinical therapy in Colorado

This booking path is dedicated to Colorado clinical therapy services. Global counseling, coaching, courses, and workshops use the main booking page.

Booking and policy rules

  • New booking allocations open 24 hours prior to the available slot.
  • Cancellations are non-refundable.
  • Rescheduling to another time on the same calendar date has no extra charge.
  • Rescheduling to a different date adds $25.

Colorado clinical service types

Availability is shared across all booking paths to prevent double-booking.

Need global services instead?

If you need counseling, coaching, courses, or workshops outside Colorado clinical therapy, use the global booking path.
