How appointment request works

The scheduling flow is designed to reduce delays and make first-session prep clear. Most clients can complete booking in a few minutes when service type and preferred times are already selected.

  • Select the service that matches your needs
  • Choose a live calendar time slot
  • Complete intake details and confirm booking
  • Receive confirmation and next-step guidance

Before you submit

  • Use an email address you monitor daily for confirmations
  • Share only scheduling details, not sensitive clinical information
  • Have two alternate times ready in case your first choice fills quickly

Important notes

Service types may have different payment and policy rules. Appointment terms are visible before final booking confirmation.

  • Some appointments are paid at booking
  • Paid sessions can be rescheduled
  • Paid cancellations are non-refundable

Response times

Appointment confirmations and routine follow-up are typically handled during office hours. Emergency care is not provided through this website.
