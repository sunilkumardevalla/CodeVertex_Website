Anxiety Therapy for Professionals

Support for high-responsibility professionals managing chronic anxiety and decision fatigue.

Who This HelpsWhat To ExpectHow Progress Is Tracked
Book SessionContact Office

Who this helps

  • Leaders under sustained pressure
  • Professionals with overthinking loops
  • Clients with stress-related sleep disruption

Common signs

  • Persistent worry before meetings or decisions
  • Difficulty switching off after work
  • Physical anxiety symptoms during routine tasks

Treatment approach

  • Trigger mapping and cognitive restructuring
  • Nervous-system regulation plans
  • Decision pacing and boundary scripts

Expected timeline

  • Weeks 1-2: symptom and trigger baseline
  • Weeks 3-6: intervention practice in real work contexts
  • Weeks 7-10: relapse prevention and sustainability

Potential outcomes

  • Improved decision clarity
  • Reduced emotional spillover after work
  • Higher confidence managing high-stakes conversations

Outcomes vary by context and participation. Progress is strongest when sessions and between-session actions are consistent.
