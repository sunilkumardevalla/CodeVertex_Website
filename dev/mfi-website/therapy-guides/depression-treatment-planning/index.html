Depression Treatment Planning

A practical framework for mood recovery, functioning support, and routine stabilization.

Who This HelpsWhat To ExpectHow Progress Is Tracked
Book SessionContact Office

Who this helps

  • Clients with low mood and withdrawal
  • People with motivation decline
  • Clients needing structured recovery steps

Common signs

  • Persistent sadness or numbness
  • Daily tasks feel difficult
  • Sleep and appetite shifts impact functioning

Treatment approach

  • Mood and thought-cycle assessment
  • Behavior activation planning
  • Routine recovery and cognitive tools

Expected timeline

  • Weeks 1-2: baseline and stabilization
  • Weeks 3-6: activation and coping systems
  • Weeks 7-10: maintenance and resilience

Potential outcomes

  • Improved emotional steadiness
  • Better daily functioning
  • Higher motivation and follow-through

Outcomes vary by context and participation. Progress is strongest when sessions and between-session actions are consistent.
