Accessibility

Accessibility statement and conformance roadmap.

CodeVertex targets WCAG 2.2 AA and continuously improves accessibility across content, motion, and interaction patterns.

Current conformance focus

  • Keyboard-first navigation and focus visibility
  • Text contrast and non-color-dependent cues
  • Reduced-motion support for animated sections
  • Semantic HTML and assistive technology compatibility

Known improvement backlog

  • Periodic manual screen-reader audits
  • Video/animation transcript alternatives
  • Continuous ARIA label review on interactive widgets
  • Multilingual accessibility QA in EN/ES content

Request accessibility assistance

If you encounter a barrier, contact accessibility@codevertex.io with page URL, device, browser, and issue details. We target acknowledgement within 2 business days.
