Board cyber brief template: what executives actually need

Focus on decisions, exposure, and timelines. Avoid technical overload in governance forums.

Required sections

  1. Current risk posture and material changes since last report
  2. Top 3 business-impact scenarios with confidence levels
  3. Control effectiveness summary and known blind spots
  4. Response readiness and unresolved capability gaps
  5. Decision asks: budget, prioritization, ownership

Use a single page summary first, then attach technical appendices for deep-dive sessions.
