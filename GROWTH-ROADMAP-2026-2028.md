# CodeVertex Enterprise Growth Roadmap (24 Months)

Date: February 19, 2026
Window: March 2026 to February 2028

## Objectives
- Build predictable enterprise lead flow.
- Convert project work into recurring retainers.
- Raise average deal size and shorten sales cycle.
- Turn content into a measurable pipeline asset.

## North-Star Targets (by Month 24)
- Monthly qualified leads: 35+
- Proposal win rate: 28-35%
- Recurring revenue share: 60%+
- Average deal size: USD 45K+ projects, USD 20K+ monthly retainer
- Payback period on marketing spend: under 4 months

## Phase 1: Foundation and Instrumentation (Months 1-3)
1. Finalize CRM pipeline stages and SLA rules.
2. Enable webhook adapter tests in production-like sandbox.
3. Publish pricing guide, CRM mapping, and trust assets.
4. Launch editorial operating cadence (2 blogs/month).
5. Set dashboard baseline: visits, CTA clicks, form conversion, lead tier mix.

Deliverables:
- CRM routing quality >95%
- 6 new high-intent content assets
- Weekly pipeline review ritual active

## Phase 2: Offer-Market Fit Expansion (Months 4-6)
1. Build vertical landing narratives (SaaS, Finance, Healthcare).
2. Run outbound partner motion with compliance advisors and MSPs.
3. Add at least 2 new public case outcomes.
4. Launch quarterly executive briefing webinar funnel.
5. Refine pricing bands using close/win and lost-reason data.

Deliverables:
- 20+ qualified leads/month
- 1 partner-sourced opportunity/week
- Retainer attach rate from projects: 25%+

## Phase 3: Revenue Engine Scaling (Months 7-12)
1. Launch sector-specific package bundles.
2. Productize incident readiness and vCISO tracks.
3. Add nurture automation by lead tier and timeline intent.
4. Publish monthly threat/risk brief and downloadable checklist updates.
5. Deploy referral incentive model for existing clients/partners.

Deliverables:
- 30+ qualified leads/month
- Proposal cycle reduced to <=14 days average
- Retainer revenue reaches 45-55% of total

## Phase 4: Brand Authority and Enterprise Expansion (Months 13-18)
1. Add multilingual campaign content (EN/ES expansion).
2. Launch annual benchmark report as flagship lead magnet.
3. Introduce account-based content for top 20 target accounts.
4. Build strategic alliances with cloud and legal advisory partners.
5. Expand case-study program to include quantified outcome metrics.

Deliverables:
- 40% of inbound from authority content and referrals
- Average deal size +20% versus Year 1 baseline
- Multi-region enterprise deal mix increase

## Phase 5: Operational Excellence and Margin Optimization (Months 19-24)
1. Automate proposal assembly for standard package scenarios.
2. Implement service delivery scorecards tied to renewal probability.
3. Optimize pricing floor and add-ons to improve gross margin.
4. Expand recurring offerings with executive governance package.
5. Build annual client success and renewal campaign rhythm.

Deliverables:
- Recurring share 60%+
- Renewal probability model operational
- Higher-margin advisory attach in >30% of enterprise accounts

## 24-Month Content Plan
- Monthly: 2 blogs + 1 case update + 1 downloadable asset refresh.
- Quarterly: 1 webinar + 1 executive brief packet + 1 partner co-marketing asset.
- Annual: flagship benchmark report + website trust/procurement refresh.

## Monetization Channels (Prioritized)
1. Primary: enterprise services and retainers.
2. Secondary: vCISO/advisory and incident readiness programs.
3. Third: paid workshops, executive briefings, and partner-led referrals.
4. Long-term: premium research/benchmark subscriptions.

## KPI Operating Scoreboard
- Top funnel: organic visits, referral visits, CTA CTR, form completion rate.
- Mid funnel: qualified-lead rate, meeting-book rate, proposal rate.
- Bottom funnel: close rate, average deal value, sales cycle days.
- Revenue quality: MRR, retainer mix, churn/renewal rate, gross margin.

## Team Operating Model
- Founder/Leadership: enterprise calls, top proposals, strategic partnerships.
- RevOps: CRM hygiene, scoring, pipeline reporting, workflow QA.
- Security Delivery Lead: scoping quality, utilization, delivery consistency.
- Content Lead: editorial calendar, SEO clusters, conversion CTAs.

## Quarterly Review Checklist
1. Which services close fastest and at highest margin?
2. Which content assets generate qualified meetings?
3. Where do deals stall (legal, pricing, scope, trust)?
4. Which partner channels are producing real revenue?
5. Which offers need packaging or pricing adjustment?
